Initial release of PrivacyFlip v1.0.0

Features:
• Automatic privacy control on screen lock/unlock
• Background service for persistent monitoring
• Material 3 design with dark theme support
• Root-based system feature control
• Quick Settings tile integration
• Panic mode for emergency privacy
• Configurable timing delays
• No tracking or analytics

Privacy Features:
• Wi-Fi control
• Bluetooth control
• Mobile data control
• Location services control

Requirements:
• Root access required
• Android 5.0+ (API level 21)
